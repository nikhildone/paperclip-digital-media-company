# QA, Approval, and Output Pack Standard

## Purpose
Ensure every output is safe, useful, and manually upload-ready.

## Output Pack Standard
Each final pack must include:
- README
- Research brief
- Content strategy
- Reel scripts
- Carousel copy
- Captions and hashtags
- Media files or fallback design briefs
- Platform preview
- QA report
- Upload checklist

## QA Checklist
- Is the hook strong?
- Is the topic relevant to SINK DINK audience?
- Is the claim safe and accurate?
- Is the caption platform-ready?
- Are hashtags relevant?
- Are files named correctly?
- Is the video duration suitable?
- Are subtitles readable?
- Are carousel slides clear?
- Is final upload checklist complete?

## Approval Stages
1. Idea approval
2. Draft approval
3. Media preview approval
4. QA approval
5. Final upload-ready approval

## Hard Safety Rules
- No automatic publishing.
- No spending without approval.
- No credential exposure.
- No final label without QA.
- No major strategy change without approval.

## Manual Upload Role
The user manually uploads final files to Instagram, YouTube, and other platforms.
